Netflix Dataset Overview

Download Dataset

This dataset contains metadata on movies and TV shows available on Netflix as of 2021. It was sourced from Kaggle.

Before analysis, we dropped rows where cast is missing to ensure accurate actor-based visualizations. This reduced the data from 8,807 to 7,982 entries.

There are a total of 36,439 unique actors in the cleaned dataset. The most frequently occurring genre is International Movies, which appears 2,574 times.

Key Columns Used

  • title: The name of the movie or TV show.
  • type: Indicates whether the title is a Movie or a TV Show.
  • cast: A list of main actors featured in the title.
  • country: Country of origin or availability.
  • release_year: The year the title was released.
  • listed_in: Categories or genres the title falls under.
  • description: A short summary or synopsis of the content.
